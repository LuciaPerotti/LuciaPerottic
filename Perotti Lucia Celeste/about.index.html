A NOTE FROM OUR FOUNDER

My journey towards healthier skin inspired me to develop products that really work, in a way that’s accessible to everyone.
Rhode is dedicated to making products based in science and great formulation, simplifying many of the mysteries and complex narratives behind efficacious skincare.
I hope these will become your go-to essentials that can live in your bathroom, be your favorite travel companion, improve your skin over time, and keep your skin happy and hydrated.

intentional skincare: Rhode is a line of curated skincare essentials. Formulated for a variety of skin types and needs with high performance ingredients, it’s a daily routine that nourishes your skin barrier over time.

our values: From limited releases, to consciously-sourced ingredients, to upcycled and recyclable packaging, we are committed to mindful skincare. Clean. Vegan. Cruelty Free. Gluten Free. Fragrance Free.
